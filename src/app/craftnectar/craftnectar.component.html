RPG Experience

This server is meant to be an RPG experience - and what kinda server would it be without levels? We have a custom leveling system that allows players to level up certian skills by practicing them. Mine ores to level mining, grow crops to level farming, etc and then reap unique benefits for leveling said skill. Complete guild quests to level your Adventure level to earn higher stats such as more health. Once you're strong enough face off in the Dungeon against waves of mobs and try to defeat the Dungeon boss!!!
